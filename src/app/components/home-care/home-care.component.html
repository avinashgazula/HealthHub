We Care for You

Qualified, professional, trustworthy, kind, and dependable. We treat all of our patients as individuals, and we understand that we’re only as good as the caregivers who work with us and who serve our clients in care. We are pleased to serve clients throughout the Halifax area of Nova Scotia.

We’re serious about the service we provide to seniors and others in our care. We’re diligent when it comes to checking references of potential caregivers — no employee is ever engaged until they undergo a thorough screening process which includes a Criminal Reference Check, and all new caregivers must be bonded and insured by the Worker Compensation Board.

We appreciate the responsibility that comes with our very hands-on role of client care. We are proud to offer our services to clients across the Halifax area of Nova Scotia, including Dartmouth, Bedford and Sackville. We know that when we treat our care providers with kindness and respect, it will be reflected in the care they provide for our clients.

Our employees have set an enviable standard for live-in caregivers that has garnered much praise from our clients. They offer a true level of consistency and comfort with the elederly on rotation, and they really become part of the family.
